Information of Different Planets




Temperatures on different planets vary a lot. Planets like Mercury (being close to the sun) can go up to 800 degrees F. But other planets like neptune (being far from the sun) has a low of -391 degrees F. This shows how much temperatures can vary just in our solar system.


Mercury's Information




Mercury is the closest planet to the sun. It is only 31.685 million miles away (in space that is little) making it a hot planet although not the hottest. The lowest the temperature goes is -230 degress F and its highest 800 degrees F.


Venus' Information




Venus is the second planet from the sun. It is 67.62 million miles from the sun and is the hottest planet in our solar system. Venus stays in around 800 degrees F all the time. It is the hottest planet because it traps heat and since it is close to the sun which makes it burning hot.


Earth's Information




Earth is the planet that we live on also the 3rd planet from the sun. Earth is 93.5 million miles away from the sun. It is the perfect temperatures for us and is known to lots of living things including plants and animals. Earth's highest temperature ever was 136 degrees F and its lowest ever is -126 degrees F. Earth has great temperatures for all living things including you and me!


Mars' Information




Mars it the 4th planet from the sun. It is 131.32 million miles from the sun. Mars has a maximum temperature of 70 degrees F because now we are getting further from the sun. Also it has a lowest temperature of 80 degrees F.


Jupiter's Information




Jupiter is the 5th planet from the sun. It is 462.24 million miles from the sun. Jupiter has a temperature of about -238 degrees F. Jupiter is the biggest planet in our solar system and is also a gas giant. It is part of the outer 4 planets which are the coldest and the biggest.


Saturn's Information




Saturn is the 6th planet from the sun. It is 886 million miles from the sun. Saturn is has a temperature of about -285 degrees F. Saturn has the most visible rings ou tof all the planets in our solar system. Its rings are made of debris like rocks and ice.


Uranus' Information




Uranus is the 7th planet from the sun. It is 1.8313 billion miles from the sun. Uranus is one of the furthest planets from the sun and is really cold. It is a bluish-green color. Uranus is -535 degrees F making it the coldest planet in the solar system.


Neptune' Information




Neptune is the 8th and final planet in are solar system (not counting dwarf planets). It is -373 degrees F. Neptune is also a bluish color like Uranus. Neptune is also the smallest gas giant.
